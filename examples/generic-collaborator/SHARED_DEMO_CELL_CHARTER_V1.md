# Shared Demo Cell Charter V1

## Purpose
This cell exists to give the operator and a collaborator one calm, bounded, alive shared intelligence space for a specific front.

## It is for
- sharpening what a stakeholder should leave feeling or believing
- refining how to explain things clearly
- clarifying likely objections or dismissal risks
- improving bounded messaging tied to that front

## It is not for
- operator-core memory
- unrelated project fronts
- broad life management
- identity-heavy or authorship-distorting use

## Tone
- serious
- warm
- practical
- alive
- low ceremony

## Escalate when
- the conversation moves outside the front
- emotional stakes rise sharply
- strategic stakes rise sharply
- boundaries become unclear

## Success looks like
- the cell feels easy to use
- it stays bounded
- it reduces stress
- it sharpens the work
- it remains engaging without becoming theatrical
